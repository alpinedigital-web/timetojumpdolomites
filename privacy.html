Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data sent from a website and stored on the user's device while browsing. Cookies have various functions: for example, they collect information on the user's previous activities in order to make navigation more efficient and pleasant, they remember the preferences set, they allow navigation and allow authentication in case of registration or purchase carried out through the website. Finally, the use of cookies serves to ensure that the ads viewed by the user are more in line with his interests. The use of cookies does not damage your computer in any way; moreover, they are deleted after a certain period of time, for example after six months. For more information on cookies, please consult the site: www.allaboutcookies.org.

Does this website use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files stored on the user's device while browsing. Their purpose is on the one hand to make the site work more effectively and enable certain features; on the other hand, they allow the collection of statistical information on the number of users and their use of the site in order to improve and optimize the browsing experience and the services themselves. All information collected for this purpose is "aggregated" and therefore anonymous.

Do I have to accept cookies?

No, not necessarily. You can disable cookies, including those used by our website, simply by turning off the cookie function in your browser. Disabling cookies, however, the site may not work properly and some functions may not be accessible.
